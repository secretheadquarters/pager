Buttons header
--------------
Pressing button connects to GND.
Use GPIO with pull-up resistor enabled.

B1  GPIO7
B2  GPIO12
B3  GPIO16

Output header
-------------
Connect red and green LED
Header connects to open-collector NPN transistor
A high signal on GPIO turns transistor on
O1  GPIO21
O2  GPIO20
